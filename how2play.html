How to play

Objective

The aim is to score as many runs as possible while completing 20 overs an innings, while avoiding getting all out.

  • Setup: Use standard four decks of cards, consisting of 52 playing cards.
  • Scoring sheet: Prepare scoring sheet with two opposing 11 players teams.
  • Players: Two or more players.

Rules and Game Play

  • Deck of Cards: 4 Deck of playing Cards containing numbered 2-10 cards are required to play the game. Face cards, Joker, Jack, Queen, King and Ace are removed from the 4 deck. The playing Deck must contain 144 Shuffled playing cards.
  • How to begin: To begin the game, bowler (card dealer) deals 6 cards from a shuffled deck, face down, one card at a time between the bowler and the batsman. The bowler collects 3 additional cards from deck to receive 9 as against 6 for the batsman. Bowler 9, Batsman 6 Cards!.
  • How to make runs: TThe batsman accumulates run during an innings based on certain algorithm - a difference between similar colour cards and number on the cards played ball by ball, and is recorded in a scorebook.
